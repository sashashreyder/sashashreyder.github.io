My Website Style Guide, May 2024

Colors

Black Olive

#3b3c36

This color can be utilized for text, background, or accent elements to enhance readability, provide a neutral backdrop, and add visual interest to web interfaces.

Bright Navy Blue

#1974d2

This color can enhance text clarity, provide dynamic background options, and infuse visual pop into different elements of web interfaces.

Brunswick Green

#1b4d3e

This color can evoke a sense of sophistication and natural allure, incorporating it into color schemes for branding, graphics, and UI elements to convey stability, growth, and a connection to the environment.

Cherry Blossom Pink

#ffb7c5

This color can infuse warmth and delicacy into designs, incorporating it into branding, illustrations, and UI elements to evoke feelings of romance, femininity, and serenity, particularly suitable for projects targeting audiences seeking softness and elegance.

Dark Lavender

#734f96

This color can create a sophisticated and calming aesthetic, ideal for backgrounds, accent walls, and branding elements, pairing well with metallics and neutral tones to evoke elegance and tranquility.

IndianRed

#cd5c5c

This is a warm, earthy hue ideal for adding depth and coziness to interiors, pairing well with neutrals and other warm colors, and conveying reliability and tradition in branding.
